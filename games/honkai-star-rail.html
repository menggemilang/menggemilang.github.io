About Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based RPG set in a science fiction universe. Players take on the role of the Trailblazer, who boards the Astral Express to explore different worlds across the galaxy. Along the journey, you'll encounter various civilizations, solve mysteries, and battle powerful enemies using a strategic turn-based combat system.

Key Features

  • Strategic turn-based combat with a unique weakness and break system
  • Rich storyline with branching paths that affect the narrative
  • Diverse roster of characters, each with unique abilities and backstories
  • Exploration of various worlds, each with distinct cultures and environments
  • Cooperative multiplayer for challenging endgame content
Game Details

Genre: Turn-Based RPG

Platform: PC, PlayStation, iOS, Android
